About Project

Embrace the journey with SpaceTravel, where each trip its a unique adventure around the universe.

This platform, crafted for the enthusiastic travelers, follows a detailed project plan.
It features distinct public and private interfaces.

Accessible to all, the public interface showcases the most exotic space locations you will ever see
along with sign-in and sign-up options, and a dynamic search tool
where you can find every trip you will ever need.

Exclusive to members, the private section opens an unbelievable experience
It includes personalized travel trips, unique profile customization,
and the ability to manage every personal space travel trip available.
Members can also engage with the community by liking,commenting,
downloading and sharing photos.

Built on Angular, the client-side emphasizes seamless navigation and interaction with unique space photos.

The backend, a Node.js and Express.js based RESTful API, ensures incredible performance.

Data handling and storage are powered by MongoDB, enhanced with Mongoose for super efficiency.
